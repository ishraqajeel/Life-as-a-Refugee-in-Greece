About Us

We are individuals dedicated to inform refugees about all the useful information they need inorder to start a new healthy life in Athens, Greece. Types of assisstences contains information about places that they to visit inorder to ask for legel, social and another advices. Moreover, some rights, rules and duties they need to consider while being in Athens. plus some links and maps for usful legel, social and health institutions and many more. Our site is directed by volunteers, who are eager to help refugees in Greece. Our target is only to direct you to do the best but we will not gurenty that you will get your porpuue, therefore, you have to follow your targets and never give up.
